Lying Motives Taxonomy
GAIN
AVOID LOSS
SELF
Self-Gain
Lying to benefit oneself
Example: Exaggerating qualifications for a job
Self-Protection
Lying to avoid personal loss
Example: Denying wrongdoing to avoid punishment
OTHER
Other-Benefit
Lying to benefit someone else
Example: "Butler lies" to maintain relationships
Other-Protection
Lying to protect someone else
Example: Lying to charity vs self (Yin et al., 2017)

Loss-avoidance
